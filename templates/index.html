ABOUT US

First Kick was developed to provide children aged 18 months – 12 years old of all abilities with fun football and multi-sport experiences. Using the UK’s passion for sport, First Kick look to develop each child, delivering experiences and memories that will last for a lifetime.

The programmes are delivered by coaches of the highest quality and those that make sessions enjoyable for all primary school aged children that attend First Kick sessions.

PRE-SCHOOL/NURSERY CLASSES

Our programmes incorporate fun games, stories and music, enabling coaches to use disguised learning techniques. This teaches children key motor, social and psychological skills whilst working on Agility, Balance and Co-ordination (ABC’s).

In addition to preparing them for school life working on numbers and colours, children will learn to develop important social skills throughout, such as sharing, taking turns and listening.
